Paid Media Staff Marketing Manager
The Real Ask

"This role is not looking for a channel executor. They want the person who owns the full paid media vision and is accountable for every dollar from brand awareness to conversion. The phrase 'revolutionize the way we go to market' signals the current approach is either stale or under-resourced — they need someone with a point of view, not someone to optimize what exists."

The Question To Ask

"Ask: 'How is media performance measured across the four products — individual product-level cost-per-acquisition targets, or a shared portfolio efficiency metric, and who owns that definition?' This surfaces whether there is internal alignment on success, reveals how much budget autonomy you will actually have, and signals you think in portfolio architecture rather than isolated campaigns."

The Unwritten Prereq

"Listed as 'a bonus,' but given the audience is tax professionals — a finite, identifiable, highly specific segment — account-based marketing thinking is almost certainly the operating model for mid-funnel and pipeline acceleration. The candidates who lose this role present ABM as a nice-to-have they have dabbled in. The candidate who wins positions it as a core competency with specific proof of how it changed audience targeting and pipeline quality."

Comp Reality Check

"The published range represents nearly $55,000 in variance — how you position yourself in the negotiation matters enormously. Do not accept an offer anchored at the midpoint without a direct conversation about equity, performance bonus structure, and annual review cycles. At this level, total compensation architecture matters as much as base salary."
